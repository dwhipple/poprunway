What is MicaPopRunway?


Fun

Mica POP Runway is a premier New York City fashion entertainment experience.

Surprising

Mica's "POP" runway feeds off of the mantra, “In New York City, you never know who you will run into or what you’ll see”.

Entertaining

A 20-minute high-energy fashion show will happen right in front of your eyes

How does it work?
Fashion in a box

You give us a day and time frame that you would like MPR to pop-up and deliver this one-of-a-kind fashion experience. Once MPR arrives, 6 models will be dressed and the show will commence. A message will be played over your sound system signifying the start of MPR. Our specialized CD will introduce the event, so no worries on explaining the fun to your guests. For 20 minutes, MPR models showcase one unique designer per show in your establishment. At the end, models may bring out a special dessert as their last look.
