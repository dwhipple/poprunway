Fashion in a Box

New York is undeniably the home of fashion’s most exciting, creative and innovative forces. MICA POP RUNWAY (MPR) combines high fashion, amazing models and trendsetting styles into one unforgettable experience.

How it works?

You provide the date, time and location and leave the rest to us! Once the MPR team arrives, six beautiful models will be dressed and ready for SHOWTIME! Each event will showcase a hot designer’s latest creations in an exhilarating show that lasts approximately 20 minutes. The interactive format allows models to engage guests and promote the host venue’s menu.

Restaraunt & Event Venues

Your customers and guests will absolutely love this sexy, engaging fashion experience featuring collections from a notable designer. We’ll also spotlight your company on micapoprunway.com and across social media channels allowing you to reach loyal fans, tastemakers and fashion enthusiasts.

Sponsorship Opportunities

We’ll customize a sponsorship package specifically for your brand. Our goal is to ensure that fans and followers across the broadcast and social media spectrums see your brand as an integral partner of MPR. A win-win for us is helping our partners promote their products and services by positively impacting the bottom-line.
