Founder - Mica Hughes

After graduating from University of Maryland College Park, with a B.A. in Communications, Mica worked as a reporter for a local news station in Maryland. Later she was scouted for modeling on the beaches of Miami where she worked for a year and then immediately took her talents to the capital of fashion in New York City. She was immediately signed with Wilhelmina Models and Ford Models in NYC, as one of their top models. Mica has lived and modeled all over the world in places such as: Paris, London, Italy, Spain, Africa and more. Working the runways for top fashion designer's such as: Oscar de la Renta, Donna Karan, Chanel, Christian Dior, Dolce & Gabbana, Ralph Lauren, Etro, Gucci, Michael Kors and others.

While pursuing her professional modeling career, Mica founded and developed Models in Motion: a model search and seminar company with emphasis on raising self-esteem among youth and educating aspiring models on the industry. This commitment took her to many cities in the US imparting confidence, integrity and knowledge. Mica didn't stop there she then decided to later open her own modeling agency, "Mica Models", now located in NYC.

As an expert in fashion and beauty, Mica found it important to also focus her talents in Esthetics and became a licensed Esthetician certified in Oncology Esthetics, Microcurrent, Microdermabrasion and Chemical Peels. Always believing that real beauty starts below the skin in more ways than one, she pushed further to open Zoli Spatique now called Mica2MSpa, an anti aging, rejuvenating skin care business. She maintains a loyal client base of models, celebrities, and business professionals.

Mica has been featured on CBS Channel 2 News numerous times educating the public on innovative and effective treatments to combat anti-aging and general skin concerns. Mica2mSpa is planning to open a new location soon.

Mica's acting resume includes many television, film, stage productions and commercials including: Spike Lee's "Girl 6", New York Undercover, Chris Rock's "The Calm Down Guy" Stage and "Central Park West," and much more.

Mica resides in NYC with her pet chinchilla named "Barack" aka Rocky. Mica loves to enjoy life to the fullest, laugh often and always finds time to enjoy her passion for singing. She is a singer, producer and song writer. You can catch her latest performance on the reunion show of Blood Sweat And Heels Season 1. #turkeybaconscrambledeggs

Mica is currently the star of Bravo TV’s hit reality show Blood Sweat & Heels, the highest rated premier show in Bravo history.
